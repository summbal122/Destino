Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in your travel packages? ▼

Our packages usually include flights, accommodations, guided tours, and some meals. Specific inclusions vary by destination, and details are listed for each trip.

Can I customize my trip? ▼

Absolutely! We offer tailor-made itineraries so you can add excursions, extend stays, or choose different accommodation types.

What is your cancellation policy? ▼

Cancellations made 30 days before departure receive a full refund. Within 30 days, cancellation fees may apply depending on the trip.

Do you offer group discounts? ▼

Yes! We offer special discounts for groups of 8 or more travelers. Contact us for details and customized group itineraries.
